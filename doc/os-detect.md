from [SO 6551006](http://stackoverflow.com/questions/6551006/):

Linux:
  * os.type(): 'Linux'
  * os.platform(): 'linux'
  * os.arch(): 'x64'

Windows:
  * os.type(): 'Windows_NT'
  * os.platform(): 'win32'
  * os.arch(): 'x64'

SmartMachine:
  * os.type(): 'SunOS'
  * os.platform(): 'sunos'
  * os.arch(): 'ia32'
